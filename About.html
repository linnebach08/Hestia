About Hestia

What is Hestia and Why Should You Use It

Hestia

Hestia is an all-inclusive, multi-functional household and life management system. Named after the Greek Goddess of hearth and home. There are many different household management, family calendar, budgeting, and to do list apps on the market today. While many of these apps are useful, the problem is that many apps are required to completely organize and structure the various aspects of your life. The purpose of Hestia is to combine all the apps and tools you currently use to thrive in a modern world into a single platform that is functional, flexible, and suited to all of your needs.

Why Use Hestia

Everyone is a part of a household, even if it is a household of one. Hestia is designed to dynamically allow for various household situations. You don’t need to have a family in order for Hestia to work, you don’t even need to live with other people. Hestia is easily adjustable to work for any kind of household you have. Hestia will allow the user to manage shared and individual calendars, create and manage to do lists, and make assignments to those in your household so that you’re always prepared for what lies ahead.

Furthermore, You will also be able to manage recipes, grocery lists, and meal plan within Hestia allowing everyone in the household to always know "What's for Dinner?". Hestia also allows you to manage your finances through its budgetary feature which allows you to add income to a budget and create and categorize expenses on that budget. Thus, Hestia helps you stay on top of your financial as well as your social life. Creating your one-stop-shop for all your household management needs.
