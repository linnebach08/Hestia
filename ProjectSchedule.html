Collaboration Plan

Communication Method

  • We will communicate primarily using Discord and Zoom

Conflict Resolution

  • In the event of a disagreement between teammates we will think objectively about the problem that lead to the conflict and compromise on a course of action that is best for the team. In this situation the third team member will act as the arbitrator.

Team Momentum

  • We will ensure team momentum by having frequent progress check-ins and through encouragement to reach out to the team when one of us gets stuck.

Failure to meet expectation

  • In the event that a teammate falls behind on their progress we will be willing to work together to get the project back on track as well as reevaluate the progress goals to ensure they are realistic. In this event we expect the team member to notifiy the rest of the team early about the failure to progress.

Budget Distribution

  • The cost of the project will be evenly distributed between all team members.
